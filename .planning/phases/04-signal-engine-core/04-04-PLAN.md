<objective>
Wire orphaned mode-specific factor weights into the pipeline and connect per-dealer spread data to the spread factor — closing both verification gaps (SIG-06 partial, multi-factor analysis partial).

Purpose: The signal engine was designed with SAVER (trend-heavy: gap=0.4, spread=0.1, trend=0.5) and TRADER (gap-heavy: gap=0.6, spread=0.3, trend=0.1) mode weights defined in modes.py, but get_mode_weights() is never called. Factor weights are hardcoded identically (0.5, 0.2, 0.3) for both modes. Additionally, compute_spread_signal([]) always receives an empty list, making the engine effectively 2-factor. This plan wires both missing connections.

Output: Fully wired 3-factor signal engine where SAVER and TRADER modes produce genuinely different signals (different weights AND thresholds), and the spread factor contributes real data to the composite score.
</objective>
